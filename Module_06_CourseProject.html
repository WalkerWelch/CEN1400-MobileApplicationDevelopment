Speed Racer

Introduction

Speed Racer came out in 2008 as a sports action comedy based on the manga series and later animated show.

Speed Racer was directed by the Wachowski Sisters who are well known for directing the Matrix series and producing V for Vendetta.

Why I love it!

Speed Racer is a fantastic film that uses a unique movie genre and creates a unique style inspired by anime but for a live action film.

The music, by Michael Giacchino, fits the movie very well and aids in the unique style and action scenes in the movie.

The action packed racing scenes are great in the movie aided by the CGI that can be over the top at times, although fitting because of the animated aesthetic.

Speed Racer also has some great heavy hitting themes of family and morality. Speed Racer's parents in the movie are some of my favorite, most wholesome parents in all of cinema.

1917

Introduction

Coming out in 2019, 1917 is a War film about an important delivery mission in World War I.

1917 was directed and produced by Sam Mendes.

Why I love it!

I usually am not a big fan of War movies but 1917 hits differently with the immersiveness of the film.

The immersive story telling of the movie comes from the fact that the film is called a "One Shot" and doesn't cut to different scenes but keeps moving in unique ways.

The movie uses cleaver techniques for snipping multiple scenes together in order to keep this "One Shot" experience together throughout the entire 119 minutes.

The story is also good and together with the way it is shot helps create a unique experience for anyone watching the movie.

Lord of the Rings: Return of the King

Introduction

The Lord of the Rings: Return of the King is the 3rd film of Lord of the Rings films directed by Peter Jackson.

Coming out in 2003, it is based on the Lord of the Rings book from J.R.R. Tolkien.

Why I love it!

Although I love all 3 of the Peter Jackson Lord of the Rings movies, the third movie hits differently as the climax finish to the story.

The music from Howard Shore is my favorite soundtrack from any movie ever. The epicness of the score adds greatly to the action packed epic.

The cast is a huge list of amazing actors and actresses that help bring this amazing story to light.

The movie won numerous awards from many different platforms including a sweep of 11 Academy Awards.
